What is Food-U-Cation?

Food-U-Cation is a non-profit organization dedicated to increasing the awareness of unhealthy eating among teenagers around the world. In the modern world, children have been consuming junk food more than ever. The goal of this initiative is to encourage healthy food in a fun, yet tasty manner.

For any interested people, this website can serve as a basis for their exploration into healthier options. I plan on expanding this project by finding other locations to partner with and inspire all teens to make an effort to eat using the right choices.
